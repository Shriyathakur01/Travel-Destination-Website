JAISALMER


Jaisalmer, known as the "Golden City," is a mesmerizing destination in Rajasthan, India, celebrated for its golden sand dunes and magnificent forts. Dominated by the majestic Jaisalmer Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, the city offers a rich tapestry of history and culture. Its labyrinthine streets are lined with intricately carved havelis, such as Patwon Ki Haveli and Salim Singh Ki Haveli.
