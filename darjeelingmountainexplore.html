Darjeeling


Darjeeling’s mountains rise majestically, cradling the town in their serene embrace. The mighty Kanchenjunga, the third highest peak in the world, towers in the distance, casting a protective shadow over the lush, emerald tea plantations that spread across the hills. The crisp, invigorating mountain air fills you with an indescribable sense of peace and awe. As the sun rises, it bathes the peaks in hues of gold and pink, a daily spectacle that leaves you breathless. Darjeeling’s mountains are not just a backdrop but a profound presence, inviting you to lose yourself in their timeless beauty and tranquility.
