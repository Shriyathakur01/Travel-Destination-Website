GOA


Goa is a vibrant coastal paradise in India, famed for its stunning beaches, lively nightlife, and rich Portuguese heritage. The state's diverse attractions range from the bustling shores of Baga and Anjuna to the tranquil beauty of Palolem and Agonda. Visitors can explore historic landmarks like the Basilica of Bom Jesus and Fort Aguada, or enjoy water sports and beach parties.
