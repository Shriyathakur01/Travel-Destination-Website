BIKANER


Bikaner, located in the heart of Rajasthan, India, is a city known for its rich history, stunning architecture, and vibrant culture. Famous for its desert landscape, Bikaner boasts several historical landmarks such as the Junagarh Fort, Lalgarh Palace, and the Karni Mata Temple. The city is also renowned for its delicious Bikaneri Bhujia and hand-embroidered textiles. With its royal heritage, Bikaner offers a glimpse into Rajasthan's royal past, making it a must-visit destination for history and culture enthusiasts.
