JODHPUR


Jodhpur, known as the 'Blue City' of India, is a vibrant destination in Rajasthan. Famous for the magnificent Mehrangarh Fort, Umaid Bhawan Palace, and its picturesque blue houses, Jodhpur offers a captivating blend of heritage, architecture, and culture. The city is also a gateway to the Thar Desert, making it ideal for those seeking historical exploration and desert adventures.
