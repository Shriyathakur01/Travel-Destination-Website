JODHPUR


Jodhpur, the 'Blue City' of Rajasthan, captivates visitors with its stunning blue-washed houses, towering forts, and vibrant bazaars. From the majestic Mehrangarh Fort to the serene Jaswant Thada and bustling Clock Tower market, Jodhpur offers a unique blend of culture, architecture, and desert charm that leaves an unforgettable impression.
