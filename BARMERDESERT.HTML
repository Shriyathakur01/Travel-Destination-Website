Bikaner, located in the heart of Rajasthan, is known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and architectural marvels. The city's highlights include the magnificent Junagarh Fort, with its intricate carvings and expansive courtyards, and the famous Karni Mata Temple, also known as the Rat Temple. Bikaner is also renowned for its camel festival, where the "ship of the desert" is celebrated with races, dances, and other cultural activities.
