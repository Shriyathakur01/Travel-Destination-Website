Srinagar


Srinagar, nestled in the heart of Kashmir, is a paradise on Earth with its serene Dal Lake, vibrant houseboats, and snow-capped mountains.
The city's Mughal gardens and blooming tulips add to its breathtaking charm,
making it a perfect blend of natural beauty and rich culture.
